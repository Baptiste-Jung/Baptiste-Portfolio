Some information about Formula 1 :

History of Formula 1

Formula 1 was created in 1946 by the FIA, the International Automobile Federation, which was presiding by Viscount Jehan. Formula 1 was inspired by European car races in the 1920s and 1930s. The first Formula 1 race took place in 1950 at the Silverstone circuit in Great Britain. This race was won by Giuseppe Farina, who also became the first Formula 1 world champion with Alfa Romeo.

Current Formula 1

Formula 1 has evolved considerably and is now considered the most prestigious category in motorsport. The 2025 season was the 76th edition of the Formula 1 championship. In 2025, there were 20 drivers spread across 10 teams. In 2026, Cadillac will join the grid, so there will be 22 drivers in 11 teams. The 2025 season ended in Abu Dhabi with Lando Norris winning his first world championship with McLaren. Formula 1 is still run by the FIA, which is now presiding by Mohammed Ben Sulayem.
