important people in teams :

Formula 1 is a sport that brings together many people, but they are not always all in the spotlight. The people who are most often in the spotlight are the drivers. That is why I will first introduce you, in this image gallery, to the 20 Formula 1 drivers during the 2025 season. These drivers do most of the work, but some tasks are performed by reserve drivers. These reserve drivers can also replace the main drivers in case of a problem. So I will also include the main reserve drivers in this gallery. The teams also include ‘team principals’, who are the main managers of a team. These managers supervise the entire team (engineers, drivers, etc.). They make the important decisions (how to develop the car, race strategies, etc.), and they are the ones who negotiate with the FIA and sponsors.
